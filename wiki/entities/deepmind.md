# DeepMind / Google DeepMind

DeepMind is an AI research laboratory founded in London in 2010 and acquired by Google in 2014. In 2023 it merged with Google Brain to form Google DeepMind. DeepMind created AlphaGo, AlphaZero, AlphaFold, Gemini, and authored the Chinchilla scaling laws.

## Key Facts
- Founded 2010 in London; acquired by Google 2014; merged with Google Brain → Google DeepMind 2023
- Created AlphaGo (2016), AlphaGo Zero (2017), AlphaZero (2018) — superhuman game play
- Created AlphaFold (2020) — solved protein structure prediction (50-year biology problem)
- Chinchilla scaling laws (2022): revised compute-optimal training to 20 tokens/parameter
- Created Gemini — Google's primary LLM family
- Demis Hassabis (CEO) won Turing Award 2024
