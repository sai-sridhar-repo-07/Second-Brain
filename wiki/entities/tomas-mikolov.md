# Tomas Mikolov

Tomas Mikolov is a Czech AI researcher best known for creating Word2Vec (2013) at Google. Word2Vec is the foundational work for all modern text embeddings and RAG systems. He later worked at Facebook AI Research (FAIR) and Meta AI.

## Key Facts
- Lead author of Word2Vec (2013) at Google Brain
- Also contributed to early RNN language models before the Transformer era
- Worked at Google Brain → Facebook AI Research → Meta AI → independent researcher
- Word2Vec's distributed representation idea is the conceptual foundation of all modern NLP
