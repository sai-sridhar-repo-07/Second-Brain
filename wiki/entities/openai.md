# OpenAI

OpenAI is an AI research company founded in 2015 in San Francisco. Originally a non-profit, it adopted a "capped-profit" structure to raise investment. OpenAI created GPT-3, GPT-4, ChatGPT, DALL-E, Whisper, and Codex. It is one of the two most influential organizations in the modern LLM era (alongside Anthropic and Google DeepMind).

## Key Facts
- Founded 2015 by Elon Musk, Sam Altman, Ilya Sutskever, Greg Brockman, and others
- Created GPT-3 (2020), InstructGPT (2022), ChatGPT (2022), GPT-4 (2023)
- Published Scaling Laws (2020) — the quantitative foundation for LLM scaling
- Microsoft invested ~$13B; OpenAI powers Microsoft Copilot products
- Several key founders/researchers left to found Anthropic (Dario Amodei, Sam McCandlish, et al.)
