# Anthropic

Anthropic is an AI safety company founded in 2021 by Dario Amodei, Daniela Amodei, and colleagues who left OpenAI. Anthropic created Claude and developed Constitutional AI (RLAIF). The company focuses on AI safety research alongside building capable AI systems.

## Key Facts
- Founded 2021 by Dario Amodei (CEO), Daniela Amodei (President), and ~10 others from OpenAI
- Created Claude (Claude 1, 2, 3, 3.5, Sonnet, Opus, Haiku series)
- Introduced Constitutional AI (RLAIF) — scalable harmlessness training via AI feedback
- Backed by Google (~$2B), Amazon (~$4B), and others
- Research focus: interpretability, alignment, scaling, and safety evaluation
