# Demis Hassabis

Demis Hassabis is a British AI researcher and co-founder and CEO of DeepMind (now Google DeepMind). He led the teams that created AlphaGo, AlphaFold, and Gemini. He won the Turing Award in 2024 for his contributions to AI research, particularly AlphaFold's solution to the protein folding problem.

## Key Facts
- Co-founded DeepMind (2010); CEO through Google acquisition (2014) and merger with Google Brain (2023)
- Led AlphaGo (2016), AlphaGo Zero (2017), AlphaZero (2018) — superhuman game play
- Led AlphaFold (2020) — solved 50-year protein structure prediction problem
- Turing Award 2024 for contributions to AI (jointly with John Jumper for AlphaFold)
- Oversaw development of Gemini — Google DeepMind's LLM family
