# Aidan N. Gomez

Aidan Gomez is a machine learning researcher who co-authored "Attention Is All You Need" while interning at Google from the University of Toronto. He later co-founded Cohere, an enterprise-focused LLM company that builds large language models for businesses.

## Key Facts
- Co-author of the Transformer paper (2017) as a University of Toronto intern at Google
- Co-founded Cohere — enterprise LLM platform focused on private deployment and RAG
- One of the youngest contributors to one of the most cited ML papers in history
