# Tri Dao

Tri Dao is an ML researcher and the primary author of FlashAttention (v1, v2, v3). His work on IO-aware attention made long context windows practical and is now the standard implementation across all major LLMs and frameworks. He completed his PhD at Stanford under Christopher Ré.

## Key Facts
- Lead author of FlashAttention (2022), FlashAttention-2 (2023), FlashAttention-3 (2024)
- PhD from Stanford University; advisor: Christopher Ré
- FlashAttention is now natively in PyTorch and HuggingFace
- Enabled 100k+ context windows for Claude, GPT-4, and others
- Now at Together AI / Princeton
