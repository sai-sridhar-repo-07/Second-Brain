# Google Brain

Google Brain was a deep learning research team at Google, founded in 2011, responsible for foundational AI research and the development of TensorFlow. It was the primary institution behind the Transformer architecture paper and numerous other landmark ML advances. In 2023, Google Brain merged with DeepMind to form Google DeepMind.

## Key Facts
- Founded 2011 at Google; merged into Google DeepMind in 2023
- Primary affiliation of most Transformer paper authors (Vaswani, Shazeer, Kaiser, Polosukhin)
- Developed TensorFlow (2015) — the ML framework used to train the original Transformer
- Produced seminal papers: Transformers (2017), BERT precursors, neural machine translation advances
