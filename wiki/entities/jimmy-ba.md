# Jimmy Ba

Jimmy Ba is a machine learning researcher and professor at the University of Toronto. He co-authored both the Adam optimizer (2014) and Layer Normalization (2016) — two of the most universally used components in modern deep learning.

## Key Facts
- Co-authored Adam optimizer (2014) with Diederik Kingma — standard optimizer for all LLM training
- Co-authored Layer Normalization (2016) with Kiros and Hinton — standard in all Transformers
- Professor at University of Toronto
