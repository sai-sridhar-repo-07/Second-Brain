# Diederik P. Kingma

Diederik Kingma is a Dutch machine learning researcher known for co-inventing the Adam optimizer and Variational Autoencoders (VAEs). He completed his PhD at the University of Amsterdam and has worked at OpenAI and Google Brain.

## Key Facts
- Co-authored Adam optimizer (2014) with Jimmy Ba — most widely used optimizer in deep learning
- Invented Variational Autoencoders (VAEs, 2013) — foundational generative model
- PhD from University of Amsterdam; later worked at OpenAI and Google Brain
- Also co-authored Glow (normalizing flows for image generation)
