# Ilya Sutskever

Ilya Sutskever is a Canadian-Israeli AI researcher who co-authored AlexNet as a PhD student at the University of Toronto under Geoffrey Hinton. He later co-founded OpenAI and served as Chief Scientist. He is among the most influential figures in the LLM era, contributing to GPT-3 and OpenAI's core research direction.

## Key Facts
- Co-author of AlexNet (2012) — the paper that started the deep learning revolution
- Co-founded OpenAI (2015); served as Chief Scientist
- Co-author of GPT-3 (2020) and numerous other landmark OpenAI papers
- Led OpenAI's Superalignment team focused on AI safety (2023)
- Departed OpenAI in 2024; founded SSI (Safe Superintelligence Inc.)
