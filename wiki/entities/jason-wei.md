# Jason Wei

Jason Wei is a machine learning researcher at Google Brain and one of the lead authors of Chain-of-Thought Prompting (2022). He also co-authored foundational papers on emergent abilities in LLMs and scaling.

## Key Facts
- Lead author of Chain-of-Thought Prompting (2022) — foundational prompting technique
- Co-authored "Emergent Abilities of Large Language Models" (2022) — catalogued capabilities appearing at scale
- Works at Google Brain (Google DeepMind)
- His CoT paper is one of the most influential practical AI papers of recent years
