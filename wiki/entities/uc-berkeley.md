# UC Berkeley

UC Berkeley is a leading public research university and a major hub for AI research. It produced Diffusion Models (DDPM, 2020), and is home to the Berkeley AI Research (BAIR) lab. Many influential AI researchers trained at or work at Berkeley.

## Key Facts
- BAIR (Berkeley AI Research Lab) — one of the top AI research groups
- DDPM (2020) — Jonathan Ho PhD under Pieter Abbeel
- Pieter Abbeel: robotics and RL researcher; co-founded Covariant
- Sergey Levine: offline RL, robot learning
- Many alumni at Google Brain, OpenAI, and Anthropic
