# Jacob Devlin

Jacob Devlin is an NLP researcher and lead author of BERT at Google AI. BERT fundamentally transformed NLP by introducing the pre-train → fine-tune paradigm with bidirectional Transformer pre-training.

## Key Facts
- Lead author of BERT (2018) — most influential NLP paper of the 2010s
- Worked at Google AI Language team
- BERT triggered an entire ecosystem: RoBERTa, ALBERT, DistilBERT, DeBERTa, domain-specific BERTs
