# Christian Szegedy

Christian Szegedy is a machine learning researcher at Google known for designing GoogLeNet/Inception (2014) and co-authoring Batch Normalization (2015). His architectural innovations in vision CNNs were foundational to the ResNet era.

## Key Facts
- Co-authored Batch Normalization (2015) with Sergey Ioffe
- Lead architect of GoogLeNet/Inception (2014 ILSVRC winner)
- Works at Google Research
- Inception modules (1×1 convolutions, parallel branches) influenced efficient CNN design
