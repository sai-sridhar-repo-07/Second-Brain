# Facebook AI Research (FAIR) / Meta AI

Facebook AI Research (FAIR), now Meta AI, is the AI research division of Meta (formerly Facebook). It produced RAG (2020), RoBERTa, BART, LLaMA series, and Segment Anything Model (SAM). It is one of the leading open-source AI research organizations.

## Key Facts
- Founded 2013; led initially by Yann LeCun
- Created RAG (2020) — Patrick Lewis et al.; most deployed production AI pattern
- Created RoBERTa (2019) — improved BERT with better pre-training
- Created LLaMA (2023), LLaMA 2 (2023), LLaMA 3 (2024) — dominant open-source LLM family
- Created SAM (2023) — Segment Anything Model for vision
- Kaiming He (ResNet author) joined FAIR after Microsoft Research
