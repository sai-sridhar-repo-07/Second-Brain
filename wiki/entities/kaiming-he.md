# Kaiming He

Kaiming He is a computer vision researcher and lead author of ResNet — one of the most cited papers in machine learning. He invented residual connections, He initialization, and PReLU. He worked at Microsoft Research, then Facebook AI Research (FAIR), and later moved to MIT.

## Key Facts
- Lead author of ResNet (2015) — Best Paper Award, CVPR 2016
- Invented residual (skip) connections — now in every Transformer and modern architecture
- Invented He initialization (Kaiming initialization) — proper weight initialization for ReLU networks
- Later created Masked Autoencoders (MAE, 2021) — self-supervised ViT pretraining
- Worked at Microsoft Research → Facebook AI Research (FAIR) → MIT
