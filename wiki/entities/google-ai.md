# Google AI

Google AI (also referred to as Google Research) is Google's broader applied AI research organization, distinct from Google Brain (which focused on fundamental deep learning research). Google AI produced BERT (2018), the T5 model, and numerous NLP advances. In 2023, Google Brain merged with DeepMind to form Google DeepMind.

## Key Facts
- Created BERT (2018) — Jacob Devlin et al.
- Created T5 (2019) — text-to-text transfer Transformer; established encoder-decoder NLP pre-training
- Created PaLM and PaLM 2 — large language models before Gemini
- Merged into Google DeepMind (2023)
