# Alex Krizhevsky

Alex Krizhevsky is a Canadian machine learning researcher who created AlexNet (2012) — the deep CNN that started the deep learning revolution. AlexNet is named after him. He was a PhD student at University of Toronto under Geoffrey Hinton.

## Key Facts
- Created AlexNet (2012) as a PhD student at University of Toronto
- AlexNet's 15.3% ImageNet top-5 error (vs 26.2% second place) launched the deep learning era
- Also created the CIFAR-10/100 datasets (widely used benchmarks)
- Later joined Google; relatively low public profile after AlexNet
