# Dario Amodei

Dario Amodei is an AI researcher and entrepreneur. He was VP of Research at OpenAI where he co-authored GPT-3 and the Scaling Laws paper. He left OpenAI in 2021 with several colleagues to co-found Anthropic, where he serves as CEO. Anthropic created Claude and developed Constitutional AI.

## Key Facts
- Co-author of GPT-3 (2020) and Scaling Laws (2020) at OpenAI
- Co-founded Anthropic (2021) as CEO along with sister Daniela Amodei (President)
- Anthropic created Claude and introduced Constitutional AI (RLAIF)
- Anthropic has raised billions from Google, Amazon, and others
