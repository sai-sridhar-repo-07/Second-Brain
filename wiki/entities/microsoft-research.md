# Microsoft Research

Microsoft Research is the research division of Microsoft, with labs in Redmond, Cambridge, Beijing, and other locations. It produced ResNet (2015), LoRA (2021), and DeBERTa, among many influential AI papers. Microsoft has also made massive investments in OpenAI (~$13B) and integrated LLMs into its product suite via Copilot.

## Key Facts
- Created ResNet (2015) — Kaiming He et al.
- Created LoRA (2021) — Edward Hu et al.; most widely used PEFT method
- Created DeBERTa (2020) — improved BERT with disentangled attention
- ~$13B investment in OpenAI; powers Microsoft 365 Copilot, GitHub Copilot, Bing
- GraphRAG (2024) — knowledge graph-based RAG method
