# Yoshua Bengio

Yoshua Bengio is a Canadian computer scientist and professor at Université de Montréal. He is one of the three "Godfathers of Deep Learning" (with Hinton and LeCun), and won the Turing Award in 2018. He supervised Ian Goodfellow (GANs) and contributed foundational work on RNNs, attention, and generative models.

## Key Facts
- Professor at Université de Montréal; co-founder of Mila (Quebec AI Institute)
- Turing Award 2018 with Geoffrey Hinton and Yann LeCun
- Supervised Ian Goodfellow (GAN inventor)
- Foundational contributions to: sequence modeling, attention precursors, VAEs, generative models
- Vocal AI safety advocate in later years
