# Jonathan Ho

Jonathan Ho is a machine learning researcher and lead author of Denoising Diffusion Probabilistic Models (DDPM, 2020) — the foundational paper for modern image generation. He completed his PhD at UC Berkeley and joined Google Brain.

## Key Facts
- Lead author of DDPM (2020) — foundation of Stable Diffusion, DALL-E 2, Midjourney
- Also co-authored Classifier-Free Guidance (2021) — key technique enabling text-to-image conditioning
- PhD from UC Berkeley under Pieter Abbeel; later joined Google Brain
- Classifier-free guidance is used in virtually every modern image/video generation system
