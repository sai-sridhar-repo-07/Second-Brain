# Jared Kaplan

Jared Kaplan is a physicist-turned-AI researcher who co-authored the foundational Scaling Laws paper at OpenAI and co-authored GPT-3. He later co-founded Anthropic. His scaling laws work is the quantitative foundation for how the AI industry makes compute allocation decisions.

## Key Facts
- Lead author of Scaling Laws for Neural Language Models (2020) at OpenAI
- Co-author of GPT-3 (2020)
- Co-founded Anthropic (2021) with Dario Amodei and others
- Background in theoretical physics — brought a quantitative, power-law analysis approach to LLM training
