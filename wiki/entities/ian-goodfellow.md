# Ian Goodfellow

Ian Goodfellow is an AI researcher and the inventor of Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs). He conceived the idea during a discussion at a bar and implemented it that same night. He worked at Université de Montréal, Google Brain, OpenAI, and Apple.

## Key Facts
- Invented GANs (2014) at Université de Montréal — one of the most cited ML papers ever
- Worked at Google Brain, then OpenAI (Director of Machine Learning), then Apple
- Author of the "Deep Learning" textbook (with Bengio and Courville) — the standard reference
- Left Apple to join Alphabet's Google DeepMind team
