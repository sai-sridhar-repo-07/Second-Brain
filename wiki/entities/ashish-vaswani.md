# Ashish Vaswani

Ashish Vaswani is a machine learning researcher and lead author of "Attention Is All You Need," the 2017 paper that introduced the Transformer architecture. He conducted this work at Google Brain and is one of the primary inventors of the mechanisms that underpin all modern LLMs.

## Key Facts
- Lead author of the Transformer paper (2017)
- Worked at Google Brain during the paper's development
- The Transformer architecture he co-created is the foundation of GPT, BERT, Claude, Gemini, LLaMA, and virtually every modern large language model
