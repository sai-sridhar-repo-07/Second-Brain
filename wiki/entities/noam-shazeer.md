# Noam Shazeer

Noam Shazeer is a machine learning researcher and co-author of "Attention Is All You Need." He worked at Google Brain and is credited with inventing several core Transformer components. He later left Google to co-found Character.AI, a consumer AI company.

## Key Facts
- Co-author of the Transformer paper (2017) at Google Brain
- Co-inventor of core Transformer components including feed-forward sublayers and architectural details
- Later co-founded Character.AI (consumer AI chatbot platform)
- Known for the "Mixture of Experts" (MoE) architecture widely used in modern LLMs
