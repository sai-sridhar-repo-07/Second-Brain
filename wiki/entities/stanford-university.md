# Stanford University

Stanford University is a leading research university in California and one of the most influential institutions in modern AI. It produced FlashAttention (Tri Dao), and is the academic home of many AI researchers. Stanford's AI Lab (SAIL) and Human-Centered AI Institute (HAI) are major research centers.

## Key Facts
- FlashAttention (2022) — Tri Dao PhD, Christopher Ré advisor
- Stanford AI Lab (SAIL) — one of the oldest AI research institutions
- Faculty include: Fei-Fei Li (ImageNet, visual recognition), Christopher Ré (Snorkel, Flash Attention)
- Many alumni at OpenAI, Google Brain, Anthropic, and other frontier AI labs
